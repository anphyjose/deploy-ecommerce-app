NAISARGGIKA ART AND SCIENCE COLLEGE, THRISSUR

Admissions 2023

Instructions to the candidate:

  • Candidate should read all the instructions carefully before filling up the online application form.
  • Candidate must meet all eligibility requirements stipulated by the University of Calicut and the Government of Kerala. Submission of the application should be made only through the link available on the college website at www.ac.in

Requirements for online registration:

  • A valid personal email ID. (If one does not have an email ID, he/she can create it under domains like gmail.com or yahoo.com free of cost.) All correspondence regarding the admission process will be made only to the e-mail id and/or phone number which the candidate provides in the application form.
  • Soft copy of passport sized colour photograph taken within the last six months, having the size of 150 px * 200px and scanned copy of signature.
